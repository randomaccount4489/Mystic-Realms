Mystic Realms

Controls: WASD to move. Press E near wild pets to tame them (Max 3 equipped). Press B to change biomes manually.

Mechanics: Biomes swap every 5 minutes and regenerate resources. Trees, rocks, and berry bushes have durability. Use the hotbar to choose what to build!
